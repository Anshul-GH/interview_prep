# MRM Policy Summary

## Purpose
This policy defines how model risk is managed for quantitative models used in trading, risk, and finance.

## Scope
Applies to all statistical and machine learning models used for pricing, trading, risk, and regulatory reporting.

## Key Principles
- Models must be documented, reviewed, and validated.
- Risk levels must be clearly defined and documented.
- Human review is required for model approvals.

## Responsibilities
- Model developers must document and test models.
- Risk owners must approve models.
- Legal and compliance must review model use.

## Review Requirements
- All models must be reviewed at least annually.
- Model changes above a threshold require revalidation.
- Model performance must be monitored continuously.
